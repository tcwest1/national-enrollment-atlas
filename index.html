    Method & data sources

    Local files expected: school directory by school year and state in json/directory/ccd_sch_directory_YYZZ_ST.json, school membership by school year and state in json/membership/ccd_sch_membership_YYZZ_ST.json (school years 2016–17 through 2024–25), and 2024–25 school point geography in json/geo/schools_2425_ST.geojson.

    State totals sum K–12 membership across every row in a state's membership file for the selected year. District totals group those same rows by LEAID. School totals group by NCESSCH. Pre-K, grade 13, ungraded, and adult-education rows are excluded when grade fields are available, matching the existing CCD District Enrollment Explorer logic.

    Change is the percent difference between the "From" and "To" school years. A district or school present only in the later year is flagged new; one present only in the earlier year is flagged closed or merged rather than assigned a misleading growth rate.

    Color domain is wider at finer grain, since small districts and schools can swing further on a small base: states are scaled to ±12%, districts to ±25%, schools to ±40%. Values beyond the domain are shown at full saturation.

    Geography: state boundaries are loaded from the public us-atlas TopoJSON CDN (not part of the local CCD files). District points use the average latitude/longitude of each district's schools from the most recent directory file; school points use the local 2024–25 geography file, falling back to directory coordinates if a state's geography file is missing.
